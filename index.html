FAQs

Common Claims Questions

How quickly should I intimate a claim?

Ideally within 24–48 hours. Contact Faithquest immediately and we'll handle the intimation on your behalf.

What documents do I need for a health insurance claim?

Typically: hospital discharge summary, bills, prescriptions, investigation reports, KYC documents, and policy copy. We provide a case-specific checklist.

How long does claim settlement take?

Cashless claims are settled during hospitalization. Reimbursement takes 15–30 days. We actively follow up to minimize delays.

Can you help if my claim gets rejected?

Absolutely. We review rejection reasons, prepare additional documentation, and help file appeals with the insurer or escalate to the Insurance Ombudsman.

Is there a fee for claims assistance?

No. Claims assistance is complimentary for all Faithquest policyholders — part of our lifelong insurance partnership.
